About Me

My name is Irene Gyebi. I am a student and aspiring data scientist with a love for storytelling. Words and data, in my opinion, are the two most powerful tools for changing the world.

I spend the majority of my time staring at a computer screen. In my spare time, I usually spend it programming and learning how to extract insights from large datasets. Data analysis, visualization, and machine learning are among my skills. I've developed a strong aptitude for problem solving, and I enjoy a good challenge now and then.

You can see some of my projects in the section below. I'll link my work to their GitHub repositories, so feel free to download and experiment with my code. The majority of my education has come from online sources. I've downloaded e-books, and spent countless hours on sites like FreeCodeCamp. I am grateful to online educators for allowing me to learn these things and for democratizing education. To give back to the community, I plan to create tutorials outlining what I've learned. I intend to write sample code for data science and visualization projects and make it available for everyone to read.
